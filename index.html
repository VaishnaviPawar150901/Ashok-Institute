Introduction

Ashok Gramin Shikshan Sanstha’s, “Ashok Institute of Engineering and Technology, Polytechnic, Ashoknagar, Tal. Shrirampur, Dist. Ahmednagar” are one of the two domains of Ashok Gramin Shikshan Sanstha’s, Ashoknagar. Ashok Institute of Engineering and Technology, Polytechnic, Ashoknagar was established in Year 2011 with Four Branches viz. Mechanical, Chemical, Electronics and Telecommunication, and Civil Engineering. Later in Year 2013 Computer Engineering was introduced. Approved by All India Council of Technical Education, New Delhi and Directorate of Technical Education, Maharashtra State and Affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai, we have successfully completed our Seven years journey with four batches been passed out.

Our college provides high-quality technical education and excellent infrastructure for student development.
